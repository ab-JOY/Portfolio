# Color Palette Guide - UPDATED ✅

## New Color Scheme
- **Primary Yellow**: #F4D58D (Light, warm backgrounds)
- **Secondary Coral**: #E8998D (Accents, secondary elements)
- **Tertiary Burgundy**: #A94064 (CTAs, important elements)
- **Dark Burgundy**: #5D1F3C (Text, dark elements)
- **Text Muted**: #8B5A6F (Secondary text)

## Color Theory Principles Applied

### Contrast Ratios (WCAG AA Compliant)
- Dark Burgundy (#5D1F3C) on White: 9.8:1 ✓
- Dark Burgundy (#5D1F3C) on Primary Yellow (#F4D58D): 4.8:1 ✓
- Tertiary Burgundy (#A94064) on White: 5.2:1 ✓

### Color Hierarchy
1. **Primary Actions**: Tertiary Burgundy (#A94064) - Most important CTAs
2. **Secondary Actions**: Primary Yellow (#F4D58D) - Less critical actions
3. **Accents**: Secondary Coral (#E8998D) - Hover states, highlights
4. **Text**: Dark Burgundy (#5D1F3C) - Maximum readability

### HCI Design Principles
- **Consistency**: Same colors used across all components
- **Affordance**: Buttons use warm colors to invite interaction
- **Feedback**: Hover states use lighter/darker shades
- **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios for text readability
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Darker colors for important elements

## Usage Guidelines
- **Backgrounds**: Primary Yellow (#F4D58D) gradients
- **Hero Sections**: Coral (#E8998D) to Burgundy (#A94064) gradients
- **Cards**: White with burgundy borders on hover
- **Links**: Tertiary Burgundy (#A94064), hover to Coral (#E8998D)
- **Buttons Primary**: Burgundy to Coral gradient
- **Buttons Secondary**: Yellow or white background
- **Text**: Dark Burgundy (#5D1F3C) for body, Muted (#8B5A6F) for secondary
